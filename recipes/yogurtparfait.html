Yogurt Parfait

A short and sweet recipes for making a delicious yogurt parfait.

Ingredients

  • Plain Greek Yogurt
  • Mix of fruits of choice
  • Granola

Steps

  1. Grab a bowl or glass and dump some Greek yogurt into it.
  2. Next, place some granola on top of the yogurt.
  3. Place your fruit mix on top of the granola.
  4. Mix everything together and enjoy!
