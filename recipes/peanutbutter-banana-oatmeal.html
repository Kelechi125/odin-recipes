Peanut Butter and Banana Oatmeal

A recipe for making a hearty and tasty bowl of oatmeal.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Plain oats
  • Peanut butter
  • Bananas
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Bag of flaxseeds
  • Bottle of honey

Steps

  1. Grab a bowl, pour some oats and milk into it, stir it together, and then heat it up in the microwave for 2:30 minutes.
  2. Take out the bowl and mix it around. When the oatmeal is to a consistency of your liking, pour some ground cinnamon into it.
  3. Next, grab your bottle of honey and squeeze some into the bowl. This will add some sweetness.
  4. Add some flaxseeds into the oatmeal.
  5. Scoop some peanut butter into the bowl and mix everything together.
  6. Slice up some bananas and place them on top of the oatmeal.
